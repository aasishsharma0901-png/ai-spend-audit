# Exact Target User

## Primary Persona

Engineering managers at Series A startups with:

* 8–25 employees
* growing engineering teams
* increasing AI tooling spend
* limited procurement oversight

These teams typically adopted AI tools incrementally:

* Cursor
* GitHub Copilot
* ChatGPT Team
* Claude Pro

without performing a centralized cost audit.

The engineering manager often feels the operational pain when:

* finance questions software spend
* multiple tools overlap
* subscriptions scale quietly over time

They are usually not the budget owner, but they are the internal stakeholder most aware of tooling inefficiencies.

---

# User Intent Before Discovery

The target user is rarely searching directly for:

```txt id="jlwmc7"
AI spend optimization
```

Instead, they search comparison and justification queries such as:

* "cursor vs github copilot which is better 2026"
* "chatgpt team plan worth it for startups"
* "how much should a startup spend on AI tools"
* "claude pro vs chatgpt plus for developers"

These searches indicate:

* uncertainty
* purchasing friction
* optimization intent

The product intercepts users during this decision-making process.

# Positioning

AI Spend Audit is positioned as:

```txt id="jlwmu3"
A fast operational cost audit for modern AI software stacks.
```

Not:

* financial software
* procurement software
* enterprise compliance tooling

The positioning intentionally emphasizes:

* speed
* simplicity
* immediate savings visibility

rather than formal expense management.

# First 100 Users Strategy (30 Days, $0 Budget)

## Week 1 — Community Seeding

Post organically inside:

* Slack communities
* Indie Hacker groups
* engineering Discords

Messaging approach:

```txt id="s7jlwm"
Built a free tool that audits AI tool spend for startups. Took me 7 days to build — curious whether it’s actually useful.
```

The framing intentionally avoids aggressive selling and instead invites feedback.

### Target

20 users

---

## Week 2 — Show HN Launch

Launch via:

```txt id="jlwmn1"
Show HN
```

Proposed title:

```txt id="jlwmr4"
Show HN: I built a free AI spend auditor for startups
```

Execution strategy:

* post Tuesday morning ET
* reply to every early comment
* collect objections and positioning feedback

### Target

40 additional users

---

## Week 3 — X/Twitter Thread

Publish a data-driven thread using anonymized audit insights.

Example framing:

```txt id="jlwmt9"
We audited 50 startup AI stacks. Here’s what we found.
```

Thread structure:

* overlapping subscriptions
* duplicate tooling
* underused premium plans
* average potential savings

### Target

30 additional users

---

## Week 4 — Direct Outreach

Send highly personalized LinkedIn messages to engineering managers.

Example outreach:

```txt id="jlwmz2"
Built a free AI spend audit tool for startup engineering teams. Tested it with a few real stacks and found meaningful savings opportunities. Happy to send the link if useful.
```

The outreach intentionally avoids spam-style automation.

### Target

10 additional users
